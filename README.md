# Motherboard Compliance Design

## Overview
Project focused on modifying and designing motherboard configurations to meet regulatory compliance requirements for PC systems.

## Objectives
- Ensure system compliance with ENERGY STAR and California Energy Commission (CEC) standards
- Address compatibility and classification challenges impacting compliance
- Support development of compliant system configurations

## Key Work
- Reclassified systems from Desktop Computers to High Expandability Computers
- Designed motherboard configurations to meet regulatory requirements
- Validated compliance through system-level testing and power measurement

## Technologies & Concepts
- System classification
- Hardware compatibility and platform design
- Regulatory compliance (ENERGY STAR, CEC)
- System validation and testing methodologies
